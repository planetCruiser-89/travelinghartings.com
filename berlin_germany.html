Return to the Homeland

Berlin, Germany | July 24, 2018

During the rise of the Nazi Regime Michelle's grandfather Peter, like many jewish people, fled Germany. Pete left his family and home in Berlin and snuck onto a ship in Genoa, Italy, and ended up in shanghai, China. Staying there for some time he then hopped on a ship headed for California, and ultimately migrating to St. Louis Missouri.
