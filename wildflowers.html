WildFlowers

Road of 52 Tunnels, Italy | July 19, 2020

While hiking in the Dolomites the end of the trail was the goal, pushing on, gasping for air, eyes forward. Obviously due to our eyes always looking forward we would miss so much around us. So we began to look at the wildflowers and doing so made the journey more fun. Looking at the wildflowers made us more present, now we try to always look for our wildflowers wherever we are, whatever we are doing.
